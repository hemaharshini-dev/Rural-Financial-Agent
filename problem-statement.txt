3. Voice AI Agent for Accessibility & Societal Impact
Problem Statement
A large portion of the global population faces barriers in accessing digital services due to literacy, language, or usability constraints.
Challenge
Create a voice-first AI agent using Vapi that improves accessibility and real-world usability in any domain of your choice (e.g., healthcare, education, public services, finance, etc.).
Your solution may explore:
Multilingual or low-literacy interactions


Context-aware guidance or decision support


Simplifying complex systems through conversation


Bridging gaps between users and essential services


Technical Direction
Incorporate Qdrant to:
Enable contextual understanding and retrieval


Personalize interactions over time


Support intelligent, adaptive responses


Goal
Demonstrate how voice AI can create meaningful societal impact, making technology more inclusive and accessible.
